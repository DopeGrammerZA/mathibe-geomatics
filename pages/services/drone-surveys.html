About This Service

Our aerial surveying services utilize cutting-edge drone and LiDAR technology to capture high-resolution data over large areas efficiently and safely, providing comprehensive spatial information for various applications. We combine aerial photography, videography, and LiDAR data to deliver detailed insights for your projects.

Key Features

  • High-resolution aerial photography and videography
  • LiDAR data collection for terrain modeling
  • Orthomosaic and digital surface model creation
  • Volumetric calculations for stockpiles and excavations
  • Infrastructure inspection and monitoring
  • Large-area mapping and surveying
  • Thermal and multispectral imaging
