About This Service

Our mining survey services provide accurate data collection and analysis for all phases of mining operations, from exploration and planning to production and rehabilitation. We specialize in providing precise spatial data that supports efficient and safe mining operations while ensuring regulatory compliance.

Key Features

  • Pit and dump surveys with volume calculations
  • Mine planning and design surveys
  • Stockpile volume measurements
  • Deformation monitoring of slopes and structures
  • Compliance surveys for regulatory requirements
  • Underground mine surveying
  • Rehabilitation and closure surveys
