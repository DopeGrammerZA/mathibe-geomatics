3D Laser Scanning & Modelling

High-precision 3D laser scanning for accurate as-built documentation and detailed 3D modeling of structures and terrain

About This Service

Our 3D laser scanning services provide highly accurate as-built documentation and detailed 3D modeling for various applications including architectural preservation, construction verification, and facility management. Using state-of-the-art laser scanning technology, we capture millions of data points to create precise digital representations of physical environments.

Key Features

  • High-resolution point cloud data collection
  • Accurate as-built documentation and verification
  • Detailed 3D modeling and BIM integration
  • Clash detection analysis for construction
  • Volume calculations and deformation monitoring
  • Historical preservation and archiving
  • Facility management and space planning
