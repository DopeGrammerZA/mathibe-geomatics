Underground Service Detection (GPR)

Ground Penetrating Radar technology to locate and map underground utilities and structures with precision

About This Service

Using advanced Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) technology, we accurately locate and map underground utilities, structures, and anomalies to prevent damage during excavation and support infrastructure planning. Our non-destructive testing methods ensure safety and efficiency in all underground detection projects.

Key Features

  • Non-destructive subsurface investigation
  • Accurate utility mapping and marking
  • Void and anomaly detection underground
  • Concrete scanning for rebar and cables
  • Archaeological investigations
  • Geological assessments
  • Depth estimation and material identification

Applications

Utility Mapping

Locate and map water, gas, electrical, and communication lines

Construction Safety

Prevent damage to underground utilities during excavation

Archaeology

Non-invasive investigation of historical sites

Geotechnical

Subsurface profiling for engineering projects
