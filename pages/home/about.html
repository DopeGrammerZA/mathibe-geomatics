Our Story

From humble beginnings to industry leadership in South African geomatics

Pioneering Excellence Since 2008

Founded by Kagiso Mathibe, a visionary land surveyor with deep roots in South Africa's construction industry, Mathibe Geomatics began as a small practice in Letlhabile, North West. What started as a one-person operation has grown into one of the most trusted geomatics firms in the region.

Our journey mirrors South Africa's own growth story. We've been privileged to contribute to major infrastructure projects, residential developments, and mining operations that have shaped our nation's landscape. From the early days of traditional surveying methods to today's cutting-edge drone and LiDAR technology, we've consistently evolved to meet South Africa's unique challenges.

What sets us apart is our deep understanding of local conditions, regulations, and communities. We don't just survey land; we build relationships and contribute to sustainable development that benefits all South Africans.
