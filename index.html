Our Story

Why we started e-KASI

Growing up in Lethlabile, we saw how many people struggled to access basic digital services. Students missing application deadlines because the nearest internet cafe was too far. Business owners losing contracts because they didn't know how to register on CSD. Job seekers paying transport to get to town just to print one page.

So we opened e-KASI – not just to provide a service, but to be a bridge. Thabang and the team bring together technology, knowledge, and genuine care for every person who walks through our doors.

  • No appointment needed – walk in anytime during trading hours.
  • We explain every step of complex registrations in plain language.
  • Affordable pricing designed for Kasi budgets, always.
  • Friendly staff who genuinely want to see you succeed.
