What We Do

Seven Specialised Geomatics Services

From underground utility detection to millimetre-accurate as-built surveys – we deliver data you can stake your project on.

3D Laser Scanning

High-density point clouds of buildings, structures, and industrial assets. Ideal for as-built documentation, heritage recording, and clash detection in BIM workflows.

Accuracy: ±2mm · Range: 300m
Learn More

GPR Underground Detection

Non-destructive ground-penetrating radar surveys to locate buried utilities, voids, and sub-surface anomalies before excavation – preventing costly damage and delays.

Depth: up to 5m · Resolution: 50MHz–1GHz
Learn More

Drone & LiDAR Surveys

Rapid large-area mapping using DJI Matrice 300 with integrated LiDAR. Cover hundreds of hectares in a single flight with sub-centimetre ground accuracy.

GSD: ≤2cm · Coverage: 200ha/flight
Learn More

Topographical Surveys

Detailed terrain models capturing contours, drainage features, buildings, and services. The foundation for every successful development application and engineering design.

Accuracy: ±20mm H | ±30mm V
Learn More

Mining Surveys

Volumetric calculations, blast monitoring, deformation surveys, and mine-plan updates. We work safely in active underground and open-cast environments.

Volume Accuracy: ±0.5% · MHSA Compliant
Learn More

Engineering Surveys

Control networks, setting-out, as-built surveys, and deformation monitoring for infrastructure projects. Tight tolerances, fast turnaround.

Setting-out Accuracy: ±5mm
Learn More

Draughting & CAD Services

Professional AutoCAD and Civil 3D draughting of survey plans, sectional title diagrams, general plans, and municipal submission drawings.

Deliverables: DWG · PDF · SHP · LandXML
Learn More
