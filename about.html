Who We Are

More Than a Survey Company – Your Project Partner

Mathibe Geomatics and Consulting is a SAGC-registered, 100% Black-owned geomatics firm headquartered in Ga-Rankuwa, Gauteng. Founded with the mission to deliver world-class spatial data services to South African industries, we combine cutting-edge technology with deep local knowledge.

We serve mining companies, property developers, civil engineers, municipalities, and government entities across Gauteng, Mpumalanga, Limpopo, North West, and the Free State. Every project, regardless of size, receives the same rigorous methodology and professional dedication.

  • SAGC (South African Geomatics Council) Registered
  • B-BBEE Level 1 – 100% Black-Owned Enterprise
  • CIDB Grade 5 Registered Contractor
  • R5 Million Professional Indemnity Insurance
  • CSD (Central Supplier Database) Registered
  • POPIA Compliant – Your data is protected

Our Values

The Principles That Guide Every Survey

Precision First

Sub-millimetre tolerances are not optional in geomatics. We invest in the best instrumentation and quality-control processes to ensure every data set is defensible.

Transparent Partnerships

No hidden fees. No scope creep surprises. We communicate proactively and deliver exactly what we promise – on time and within budget.

Responsible Practice

We follow POPIA, comply with environmental legislation, and operate safely in all environments – from high-voltage substations to active mine shafts.

Technology Leadership

We continuously invest in the latest survey technology – LiDAR, GPR, drone photogrammetry – so our clients benefit from faster, more accurate, and more cost-effective solutions.

Transformation

As a 100% Black-owned enterprise, we are committed to meaningful economic transformation in the geomatics sector – uplifting communities through skills development and employment.

Client Protection

Our insurance, rigorous QC processes, and SAGC registration ensure that if anything goes wrong, you are fully protected – legally and financially.
