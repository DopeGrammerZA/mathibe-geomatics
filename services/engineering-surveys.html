Engineering Survey Services

Engineering surveys support the full lifecycle of infrastructure and construction projects – from initial site control establishment through design, construction setting-out, progress monitoring, and final as-built verification. Every phase depends on accurate spatial data to keep your project on budget, on time, and within specification.

Mathibe Geomatics provides the tight tolerances demanded by civil and structural engineers while delivering the speed that busy construction programmes require. Our CIDB Grade 5 registration and insurance make us a reliable partner for government and private sector infrastructure alike.

Control Network Establishment

Primary and secondary control networks tied to national datum for consistent reference throughout construction.

Setting-Out

Precise positioning of foundations, piles, columns, kerbs, road alignments, and pipeline centre-lines.

As-Built Surveys

Post-construction verification surveys confirming work was built to design – required for sign-off and handover.

Deformation Monitoring

Periodic or real-time monitoring of structures, retaining walls, embankments, and bridges for settlement and movement.

Project Case Study

CASE STUDY · RUSTENBURG, NORTH WEST

14km Road Rehabilitation – Setting-Out & As-Built

A civil contractor was rehabilitating 14 kilometres of a provincial road. They required weekly setting-out of kerb lines, drainage structures, and pavement layers, plus monthly as-built surveys for payment certification.

Mathibe established a control network covering the full 14km corridor and provided weekly setting-out crews, delivering staked points within ±5mm of design. Monthly as-built surveys confirmed pavement layer depths and drainage grades for the provincial client's payment certificates.

Result: Zero rework claims. All as-built tolerances met. Project completed on time.
