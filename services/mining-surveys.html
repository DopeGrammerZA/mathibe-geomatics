Mining Surveys in South Africa

MHSA-compliant mine surveying for open-cast and underground operations. Volumetric accuracy your auditors trust, delivered by the next morning after fieldwork.

Mining Survey Services We Provide

Mathibe Geomatics provides the full spectrum of geomatics services required by mining operations – from regulatory mine-plan surveys to daily operational control and monthly stockpile reporting. We work safely in active environments, hold appropriate safety certifications, and understand the legislative framework governing mine survey in South Africa.

Our surveyors are experienced in both open-cast and hard-rock underground environments across commodities including platinum, gold, coal, chrome, and aggregates. We serve mines across Gauteng, Limpopo, Mpumalanga, North West, and Free State.

Stockpile Volume Calculations

Monthly drone LiDAR or total station surveys with ±0.3–0.5% volumetric accuracy. Reports ready for auditors.

Mine-Plan Updates

MHSA-compliant mine plan updates submitted to the DMRE on required schedules. Underground and surface plans.

Blast Monitoring & Control

Pre- and post-blast surveys for face advance measurement, overbreak/underbreak analysis, and fragmentation assessment.

Deformation Monitoring

Slope stability monitoring, subsidence surveys, and pit-wall deformation analysis for geotechnical compliance.

Project Case Study

CASE STUDY · MOKOPANE, LIMPOPO

Monthly Stockpile Volumetrics – Active Platinum Mine

A platinum mining operation required monthly stockpile volume reports accurate enough to satisfy their external auditors and DMRE reporting requirements. Previous contractor's manual surveys had ±3% inaccuracies that were causing revenue reconciliation issues.

Mathibe deployed a drone LiDAR system for monthly surveys. The result: ±0.3% volumetric accuracy confirmed by independent auditors, reports delivered by 08:00 the morning after fieldwork, and an 18-month ongoing contract renewed twice.

Result: Audit-grade accuracy. Revenue reconciliation resolved. 18-month ongoing contract.
