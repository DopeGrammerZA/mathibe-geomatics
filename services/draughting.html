Professional Draughting Services

Survey data is only as valuable as the drawings that present it. Our experienced CAD draughtspersons – who also understand survey methodology – produce clear, accurate, and professionally presented drawings that meet the requirements of municipalities, the Surveyor-General, the Deeds Office, and engineering clients.

Whether you need a topographic survey plan for a development application, a general plan for Deeds registration, or a Civil 3D surface model for an engineering team, we deliver drawings that are right the first time – saving you time, rejection risk, and resubmission costs.

Project Case Study

CASE STUDY · CENTURION, GAUTENG

180-Unit Residential Estate – Sectional Title Diagrams

A property developer required sectional title scheme diagrams for a 180-unit residential estate in Centurion. Previous surveyors had submitted diagrams that were rejected twice by the Tshwane Municipal Planning Department due to non-compliance with municipal formatting requirements.

Mathibe's draughtspersons re-produced all diagrams from the survey data, ensuring full compliance with Tshwane's submission standards. The application was approved on first submission within eight weeks.

Result: First-submission approval. Client avoided further delays on a time-sensitive development.
